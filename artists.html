Popular Bands

These artists helped shape nu-metal by combining heavy guitars with hip-hop rhythm, intense vocals, electronic textures, and personal lyrics.

Slipknot

Slipknot became known for chaotic live performances, horror-inspired masks, and a sound built around aggression, percussion, and dark emotion. Their music helped make nu-metal feel dangerous, theatrical, and unforgettable.

Linkin Park

Linkin Park blended rock, rap, electronic sounds, and emotional songwriting. Their music reached listeners who connected with themes like frustration, loneliness, pressure, and healing.

Korn

Korn is often seen as one of the pioneering bands of nu-metal. Their low-tuned guitars, unusual bass tone, and raw lyrics helped create a darker and more personal style of heavy music.

Deftones

Deftones brought a more atmospheric side to heavy music. Their songs often shift between quiet, dreamy sections and explosive guitar-driven moments, giving the genre more emotional range.

Limp Bizkit

Limp Bizkit emphasized the rap-rock side of nu-metal with loud guitars, turntable energy, and catchy hooks. Their music shows how the genre crossed into mainstream pop culture.
