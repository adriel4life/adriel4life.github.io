What Makes Nu-Metal Different?

Nu-metal is a style of alternative metal music that became quite popular in the late 1990s and early 2000s. The genre has attributes like guitar riffs with influences from hip-hop, grunge, industrial music, funk, and electronic music. You name it, nu-metal music is extensive and has borrowed from a variety of different genres. Many songs focus on personal struggle, anger, and emotional release.

Common Features

Heavy Riffs

Guitars are down-tuned for lower and heavier music, rhythmic, and focused on groove instead of long solos.

Rap Influence

Some bands do like to rap during specific sections, spoken rhythm, and hip-hop production elements are also evident.

Emotional Lyrics

If you listen to nu-metal music, you will notice that the lyrics often discuss pain, pressure, confusion, and anger.

Visual Identity

Along with the musical aspect, fashion, masks, album art, and live-show energy are major parts of the culture. Involving yourself in the scene may sometimes go beyond just the music.
