Welcome to Nu-Metal Hub

Nu-Metal Hub is a simple music discovery website focused on alternative and nu-metal music. The site introduces listeners to major bands, memorable songs, music videos, and the history behind the genre. It is designed for students, casual listeners, and music fans who want a clear starting point without being overwhelmed.
