Welcome to Nu-Metal Hub

Nu-Metal Hub is a simple music discovery website focused on alternative and nu-metal music. This site introduces listeners to major bands, memorable songs, music videos, and the history behind the nu-metal genre. Students, casual listeners, and music fans who want a clear starting point without being overwhelmed are all welcome.
